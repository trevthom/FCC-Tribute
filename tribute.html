A time line of Bitcoin-related events

  • October 31, 2008 - The Bitcoin white paper is released
  • January 3, 2009 - The genesis block is mined
  • April 18, 2011 - The first alternative cryptocurrency, Namecoin, is announced
  • November 28, 2012 - The Bitcoin block reward halves for the first time, dropping from 50 to 25 bitcoin
  • February 2015 - A draft of the Lightning Network is published, which would go on to enable cheap, fast micropayments of bitcoin
  • August 2015 - The first Bitcoin hard fork is launched, creating Bitcoin XT
  • January 3, 2017 - Bitcoin price breaks $1,000 for the first time
  • June 22, 2019 - Bitcoin price breaks $10,000 for the first time
  • June 9, 2021 - El Salvador becomes the first country to make Bitcoin legal tender
  • April 28, 2022 - Central African Republic becomes the second country to make Bitcoin legal tender
